About

Hello, I'm Abubakar Saddique, a dedicated and skilled professional with over two decades of experience in the pharmaceutical manufacturing industry. I specialize in setting up machines, electrical work, and overseeing production cycles to ensure efficient and quality-controlled operations. Since starting my career as a Blister Operator at Rasco Pharma in Lahore, I've progressively taken on more responsibilities and leadership roles, demonstrating my ability to enhance production efficiency and maintain high standards of quality control. My journey has taken me through several reputable pharmaceutical companies where I have honed my skills and contributed to successful production outcomes. With a solid educational foundation in mechanical engineering from Gov. Technical College Raiwand, and hands-on experience in various supervisory roles, I am well-equipped to tackle new challenges and contribute to a competent team. My objective is to leverage my extensive experience to further develop my skills while ensuring optimal production efficiency and quality in every task I undertake.
